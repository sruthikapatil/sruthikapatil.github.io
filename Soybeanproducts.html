Here are some products made from soybeans: Tofu A cooked soybean product that comes in firm and soft varieties. Tofu can be baked, broiled, grilled, fried, or eaten raw. Soy milk A whole soy food that may have antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and heart-protective properties. Tempeh A fermented soybean product with a dense, chewy texture and a nutty, earthy flavor. Edamame A type of soybean that is harvested when green, about 30 days before the pods turn brown and become dry. Soy nuts Soybeans that are soaked in water, drained, and then baked or roasted. They can be used in place of nuts. Soy sauce A brown, salty liquid condiment made by fermenting soybeans or breaking them down with acid. Soy flour A gluten-free flour derived from ground soybeans that can be used in baked goods like cookies and quick breads.
